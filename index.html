About Us

The main vision of this society is to cultivate innovativeness in the students and create a safe space for likeminded individuals to share and develop ideas. This will be archieved through fostering essential problem solving skills and techniques required in this new age of fast technological advancement.

The society will be an environment where coding is an easy and fun task and will dismantle the stereotype that makes programming be seen as difficult and impossible amongst students by creating an engaging, interactive arena.

Some of the core objectives of this society are as follows:
 

  • Sharpen students' skills, from what they learn in class to what they do extracurricular. The club aims in providing more practical projects for the students to engage in order to be effective problem solvers.
    •  
  • Fostering group coding projects. In the tech industry, apps and software is built by teams of people, thus the societys' objective is to build the spirit of collaboration with and amongst students and foster specialization in the different types of programming, be it backend or database development.
    •  
  • Providing outside events and speakers for students to see the concepts learned in class in action through various professionals who will translate theory into practicality
